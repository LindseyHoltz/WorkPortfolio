About

Here is some more info about me!

Background

For as long as I can remember, I have always had a fasination with animated shows and movies. Like many others, I grew up watching Disney and I was mesmerized by the magic of animation. I knew that I wanted to have a job creating content for others. I started drawing very young, and took as many art classes and oppurtunities as I could. My inspiration comes from Disney, Pixar, and Dreamworks movies, as well as many of my favorite animated tv shows. I am still learning and growing my skills everyday.

Education

I graduated from Normal Community High school in 2020, and I am currently studying at Illinois State University with a major in Creative Technologies. I have a large range of skill sets including practice in animation, character modeling, drawing, ceramics, web design, motion graphics, graphic design, and sculpture. I have had experience with different programs and software including many Adobe Programs and Maya animation software.
